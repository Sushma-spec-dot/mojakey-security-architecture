## What Privacy Protection Means

Privacy protection means limiting exposure of personal and sensitive information so that only the right users can access the minimum data needed for a task. In Mojakey, this includes parent details, child profiles, attendance history, and payment-related information.

## Why It Matters in Mojakey

Mojakey handles family data, child-related workflows, institution operations, and payment-related records. Because this information is sensitive, the platform should protect it in storage, in transit, and in user-facing workflows.

## Privacy Protection Measures

- Show only the minimum data needed for each user action.
- Restrict access based on role and tenant context.
- Protect sensitive data in transit and at rest.
- Limit access to identity documents or verification data.
- Record sensitive administrative access through audit logs.

## Risks if Privacy Protection Is Weak

If privacy protection is weak, parent or child information could be exposed to the wrong users, institutions could see data they should not access, and sensitive records could be mishandled. This would create trust, security, and compliance risks for the platform.
